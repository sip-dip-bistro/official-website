About Us

Sip and Dip Bistro is a modern restaurant and cocktail lounge located on Thika–Mangu Flyover Road. Known for serene ambiance, elegant décor, and exceptional service.

As the sun sets, Sip and Dip transforms into a vibrant nightlife destination with a sound-proof underground club.
